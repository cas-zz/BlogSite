    Activities

    How I Ended Up in Information Systems

    I am really into engineering or architecture course, so I tried to apply at my University since it offers the course that I wanted and no tuition fees. As I tried to proceed to the application I saw that there are no available slots anymore of the engineering or architecture course. Due to unavailability, I tried to apply on BSCS or Computer Science. Unfortunately, I got waitlisted and suddenly received an e-mail that I got redirected to Information Systems. This is where my programming journey begins.

    Challenges and Struggles

    Learning programming was not easy in the beginning. I remember the first time I tried C, my code was full of errors, and I couldnt figure out what went wrong. I spent hours on one tiny issue only to realize I missed a semicolon. But those small victories, like successfully debugging my first error or making a button actually do something, kept me motivated. I also found some great resources like W3Schools, which broke down concepts in a way I could understand.
